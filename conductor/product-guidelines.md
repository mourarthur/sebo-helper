# Product Guidelines

## Visual Style
- **Utility-First & Minimalist:** The UI should prioritize high contrast and readability. Large text and a clean layout are essential to ensure the app is usable in various lighting conditions typical of used media stores.

## OCR Interaction
- **Batch Processing:** When a photo is uploaded, a loading spinner will indicate processing. The final list of extracted titles will be displayed once the entire image has been analyzed. This avoids distracting the user with intermediate, potentially incorrect results.

## Feedback and Visualization
- **Match Highlighting:** Titles that match an item on the wishlist must be visually prominent. Use high-contrast colors (e.g., bright green) to ensure matches are immediately identifiable at a glance.

## Photo Input
- **Flexible Upload:** Provide a clear drag-and-drop area for images, making it easy to test with pre-captured photos or use from a desktop environment while maintaining a path toward a mobile-friendly capture button.
